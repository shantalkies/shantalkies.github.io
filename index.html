About Us

We Design, manufacture and Supply |

Brighto Fashion Valley is a professional Garments merchandising manufacturing company commenced in 2020. We Design, manufacture and export PK Polo, T-Shirt, Sports Kit, Jacket, Hoodies, Jersey, Tracksuit, Panjabi, Shirt, Pant, Backpack, laptop bags, tote bags, duffle bags, portfolio, tool bags, cooler bags, drawstring bag, Luggage, Ladies Handbag, Office Bag, etc.

We are regularly exporting bags to a number of globally renowned buyers of EU, USA, UK and Asia.

We believe in high volume sale with low profit margin and our product price is remarkably less than other country current standard manufacturing price. We can ensure standard quality and on time shipment with competitive price since we have the advantages of own sourcing office in China, Bond License & duty free facility for Import raw materials, low labor cost, experienced and technically sound manpower, advanced production equipment and facilities and required compliance certifications.
