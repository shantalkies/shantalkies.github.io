About Us

We Make and Produce |

Shan Talkies is a dynamic force in the realm of digital entertainment, captivating audiences with its rich and diverse array of content. With over a decade of experience in the film and media industry, Shan Talkies has established itself as a leading player in Bangladesh and India. Committed to delivering premium entertainment experiences, Shan Talkies offers a wide range of content including movies,web series, documentaries, and original productions, catering to the varied tastes of viewers.

With a vision to revolutionize the digital entertainment landscape, Shan Talkies embraces innovation, creativity, and technology to create unforgettable experiences that resonate with audiences worldwide.

As a proud member of the Movie Director Associations, Shan Talkies continues to push boundaries, shape the future of digital entertainment, and inspire audiences with its captivating storytelling and immersive content.
